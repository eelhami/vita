A Curated Reading List

Vision-based
Task Assistants.

A growing, opinionated collection of recent and foundational work on systems that watch what we do and help us do it better: temporal action segmentation, progress estimation, mistake recognition, AR-based guidance, and the egocentric perception that makes it all possible.

    § 03

    About this list

    This reading list is maintained as a research resource for students, collaborators, and anyone curious about the field of vision-based task assistance. It collects work that has shaped the area, plus recent papers worth knowing.

    The list is intentionally curated rather than exhaustive. Papers are chosen because they introduce useful problems, datasets, methods, or framings — not by citation count alone.
